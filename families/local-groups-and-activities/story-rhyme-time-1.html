Story & Rhyme Time

Songs to sing, stories to share, and learning through play.

Story & Rhyme Time has been specially designed to help develop your child's language and communication skills.

Together, you will enjoy learning new songs, dancing, and making friends.

We'll also give you some activities for you to try at home.

Bulwell:

  • 10am-11am for families of children 0-4
  • 11.30am-12.15pm for babies, just for babies - non-walkers only.

Aspley:

Wednesdays, Strelley Library

  •  9.45 - 10.15 for babies, non walkers only
  • 10.30 - 11.15 for toddlers (plus baby siblings)

Special police visit event on 7th August session with police car, mini dress up, 'how the police can help us' talk, mini bleep test and information for parents

Are you an SSBC Family?

We provide services to families in Nottingham who live in the areas of Aspley, Bulwell, Hyson Green & Arboretum and St Ann’s. Check if you’re in an SSBC area.

Are you an SSBC Family?
You do not live in an SSBC area. We prioritise our groups and services for families in SSBC areas. If you are looking at SSBC groups, you can contact the Family Mentor hub closest to you to find out if they have spare spaces.
