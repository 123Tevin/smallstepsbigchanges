Case Study
Steph

Before volunteering, apart from a weekly play group and the school run, Steph didn’t get out very much. Her mum lived on the other side of Nottingham and her husband worked really long hours.

Case Study
Kirsty

Kirsty was injured while training to be a midwife. But she didn't let that get in the way of her dream of working with families and children. She re-evalated her plans and re-trained to become a Family Mentor.

Case Study
Luan

In March 2020, the Covid19 pandemic forced everyone into lockdown. Luan Vickers, Bulwell Family Mentor, found herself working in a very different way to support families.

Are you an SSBC Family?

We provide services to families in Nottingham who live in the areas of Aspley, Bulwell, Hyson Green & Arboretum and St Ann’s. Check if you’re in an SSBC area.

Enter your postcode like this: NG1 3FB

Are you an SSBC Family?
You do not live in an SSBC area. We prioritise our groups and services for families in SSBC areas. If you are looking at SSBC groups, you can contact the Family Mentor hub closest to you to find out if they have spare spaces.
